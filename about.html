About Us

We Offer Value

Ilasan Property Development and Investment Limited is a private investment company inspired to create a new city within lekki, Lagos. Ilasan has an interest in real estate, Construction and project management effectively run by seasoned and highly motivated professionals.

Our Mission - To offer a new lease of life and prosperity to the people of Orile Ilasan community.

Our Vision - To transform Ilasan into a world-class future-proof city and the ultimate destination for leisure, entertainment, and comfort.

Our Mandate - To alleviate poverty and provide better livelihood for all Ilasan indigenes and the entire stakeholders in the Ilasan development project.
